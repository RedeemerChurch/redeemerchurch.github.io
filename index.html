Who we are

We are a community of people seeking to follow Jesus Christ, to share our lives with other members of the Church and proclaim His name locally in a way that brings God glory above all else. We gather regularly to encourage each other, celebrate God's undeserved grace and to grow as one in knowledge of God. As a Church, we base our teaching and services on the following foundations.

We are christian

We believe in the life of Jesus Christ and strive to follow the teachings as presented in the Bible.

We are evangelical

We belive and proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ that He died to redeem His people from their sins.

We are missional

We actively seek opportunites to share the gospel locally, nationally and internationally.

We are reformed

We acknowledge God's sovereignity over His creation and that all things occur for His glory alone.

For a more indepth explanation of the above four points, please head here.

Redeemer Youth

Just like with any community, youth are essential to Redeemer Church. To this end we run a youth ministry focused on meeting together in a small group format once a week, where we meet together to encourage one another, hear from God through His Word and fellowship with one another.

The R19 group run a separate facebook page, where they push any updates and details for any upcoming event. The button below will take you to that facebook page.
